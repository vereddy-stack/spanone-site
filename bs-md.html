BS-MD

Combined BS-MD Admissions Counseling

Advising for direct BS/MD, BA/MD and BS/DO admissions — programs that take a student from a bachelor's degree straight into medical school, in 6, 7, or 8 years depending on the program.

Direct med programs allow students to go from earning a BS or BA degree as an undergraduate directly to medical school to earn a Doctor of Medicine (MD) degree. Gaining admission to these programs is extremely competitive, even for students with a high GPA, test scores, and strong extracurriculars. Some of the direct med programs accept fewer than 10 students.

Starting early, and working with one of our BS/MD counselors to create a clear strategy, will help position your student to have a strong profile that gets noticed by admissions committees. Our experienced counselors have successfully helped over 90% of qualified students get accepted to at least one BS/MD or other direct med program.
