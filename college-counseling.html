Services

College Counseling Services

Your student will work with a college advisor who will guide them step-by-step from start to finish in the college planning and application process.

You get a personalized college application plan:

  • A written plan (timeline + milestones)
  • A best-fit list (safety/target/reach with rationale)
  • Essay leadership (topic strategy → drafts → final polish)
  • Execution support (deadlines, applications, recommendations)
  • A competitive public university strategy and a private, Ivy-level strategy

Needs Assessment

We consider each student's strengths, achievements, interests, and areas for improvement to create a strategic plan for finding colleges that are the best fit.

Interview Prep

Mock interviews so you can feel confident and come up with thoughtful questions of your own — showing colleges you're genuinely interested in attending.

Career Counseling

Our career counselor works with students through tests and questions to find the major and career direction that best suits them.

College List Development

A balanced list of safety, target, and reach schools — matched to location, major, class size, activities, politics, housing, and financial aid.

Extracurricular Activities

Our advisors brainstorm with students about their interests and advise on extracurriculars that strengthen a college application.

Summer Internships & Programs

Standardized Testing

Essays

Project Management
