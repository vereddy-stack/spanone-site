About Us

Our Expert Counselors Will Guide Your Student to College

We know how to make your application stand out from the competition. SpanOne successfully works with students at all levels.

We are experts at identifying colleges and universities that are the best fit for each student and know what it takes for outstanding students to get accepted into the Ivy League and the nation's most competitive colleges and universities, including BS/MD direct medical programs and other dual-degree programs.

Applying to college can be overwhelming, confusing, stressful, and filled with inaccurate information from parents and peers. We eliminate all of this anxiety and misdirection by managing the entire college planning and application process from start to finish. With today's college application process more competitive than ever, combined with rising tuition and myriad financial aid options, it's critical to begin planning in the early high school years.

SpanOne college advisors are a group of experienced college counselors and admissions consultants who help students — and their parents. We turn a chaotic application process into a clear plan, so students stand out and families feel confident.

About the Founder

Vasu Eda — Founder

Vasu Eda's goal in founding the company, and what he continues to ensure it achieves, is to help students reach their college goals: finding the best-fit college where they will thrive, guiding them with a plan so they're positioned to get accepted, and helping them obtain the best financial option to pay for a college education.

Vasu is the author of Get Job Ready: How to Land Your Dream Job Out of College, published by Penguin Random House. He is an internationally recognized expert on youth employability and regularly speaks at colleges on how students can gain valuable job experience and successfully transition from college to career.

Vasu's professional journey includes founding Boniva Software, an on-demand talent management and e-learning company later acquired by SSA Global. He has also shaped corporate strategy and led mergers and acquisitions at Lucent Technologies and Intel Corporation.

He holds an MBA from Columbia University, an MS in Computer Science from Stevens Institute of Technology, and a BE in Mechanical Engineering from Shivaji University. Vasu lives in the San Francisco Bay Area and has firsthand experience with the college application process, having guided all three of his children — one a UC Berkeley graduate and two Stanford graduates — through it successfully.
